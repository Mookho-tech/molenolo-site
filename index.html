About Our Farm

Molenolo Poultry Farming is a thriving agricultural enterprise based in Bloemfontein, specializing in high-quality layer chickens and farm-fresh eggs. Established with a passion for poultry excellence, we combine modern farming techniques with sustainable practices to deliver superior products to our valued customers.

Our farm operates with strict quality control measures to ensure the health and productivity of our chickens, resulting in consistently fresh, nutritious eggs. We take pride in contributing to food security in our community while maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare.

Our Mission

To be Bloemfontein's most trusted poultry provider by delivering exceptional quality products with reliable service, while maintaining sustainable farming practices that benefit our community and environment.
